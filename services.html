Energy Risk Management

The Oil refineries& Marketing companies profitability is affected by crude oil volatility and the spread movements. The power generation utilities profitability is affected by volatility in merchant power tariffs, low evacuation& rising fuel cost. The Energy consumers are also exposed to volatile energy prices. Apart from price risk, there are other important risks specific to energy markets for example delivery risk in case of transmission congestions, basis risks, volumetric risks. We believe that Energy markets are different, with different pricing system and risk characteristics. Our deep understanding of Energy markets, types of risks, risk drivers, helps us identify the risk factor, quantify its impact and suggest suitable risk mitigation measures, for our clients.

We assist our clients in

  • Scenario generations, quantifying the probable impact of risk factors movement.
  • Deciding the risk tolerance level & the budget for risk management
  • Selection of appropriate hedging instruments and their expected payoff
  • Computation of optimal hedge ratios
  • Computation of Market Risk metrics
  • Computation of Credit Risk metrics
  • Computation of Operational Risk metrics
  • Selection of Energy Risk control strategies
  • Generation of Actionable Energy Risk reports
